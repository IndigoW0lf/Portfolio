Homeschool Haven

Project Info:

A web-based program that allows you to track your students, their subjects, and the hours they have put into studying, along with a calendar and assignment tracking section. You can add multiple children, track hours across various subjects over various time-frames, and also keep track of resources for teaching current and upcoming assignments.

Details:

  • Project:Hackathon Entry for HackMerced 2022
  • Awards:First Place for best UX/UI Design
  • Technologies:HTML5, Bootstrap, CSS3, JavaScript, Grunt.js & more
  • Date:March 4-6, 2022
  • URL:www.homeschoolhaven.tech
