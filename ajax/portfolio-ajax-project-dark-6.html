Storytime UX Design

Project Info:

I utilized UX Design principles to conduct user research. Following this, I wireframed and prototyped various layouts for our website pages. Once that was done, there was a user journey map constructed as well as a branding board to establish a flow as well as a look and feel for our final capstone project.
