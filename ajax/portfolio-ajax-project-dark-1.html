Breaking Through | Resources and Mentorship for Marginalized Technies

Project Info:

A website created to provide resources and mentorship opportunities for marginalized people in the tech world. The experiences that we go through as marginalized women or enbies in tech are not unique to us alone. Each one of us has experienced negativity and doubt along our journey. Whether you are just starting your journey into tech, or have had a long career in the industry, your experiences are valuable and should be shared.
